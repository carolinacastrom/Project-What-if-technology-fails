Social Media, Communication and Conditional Thinking

Credits

General information about this educational resource
Title What If Technology Fails?
Description Students reflect on the use of technology and social media in their daily lives and in digital communication. Based on real or hypothetical situations, they will analyze issues such as: misunderstandings on social media, technical failures, excessive use of technology, difficulties in online communication. Through these situations, they will work on conditional sentences in order to: describe situations, analyze consequences, propose solutions.
