What If Technology Fails?

Social Media, Communication and Conditional Thinking

Student support

By using this Padlet, “Conditional Thinking in the Digital Era”, you will find a selection of useful resources to help you during the project. It includes videos, games, vocabulary activities and grammar explanations about conditionals, social media and digital communication. You can use these materials to review, practise and improve your understanding at your own pace, both in class and at home, so you feel more confident when completing the final task.
