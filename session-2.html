What If Technology Fails?

Social Media, Communication and Conditional Thinking

Session 2

Designing your presentation

In this second session, you will move from exploring ideas to creating your final group project. You will review key grammar points, especially conditional sentences, and then apply everything you have learned to design a Canva presentation about real digital problems related to social media and technology. Finally, you will present your work to the class and take part in a voting activity to reflect on and evaluate your classmates’ presentations.

Planning the presentation

Duración
75:00
Group work

Create a Canva presentation about a situation related to social media, technology or digital communication.

You must include:

a description of a problem
its causes
its consequences
possible solutions
conditional sentences (zero, first, second and third conditionals).

Use the section Final task as help to complete your task.

Oral presentation & voting

Each group will present their project orally to the class using their Canva presentation as a guide. After all presentations, you will vote for the best one by using Mentimeter.
